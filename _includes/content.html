About Merlink

Merlink is a simple VPN client for Meraki firewalls.

Why?

  • Meraki does not have a native VPN client
  • Windows likes to break VPN configs after updates
  • Not having a VPN client is a common pain point

Features

  • Setup/diagnose a VPN connection with a few clicks.
  • Connect to any firewall in your organization.
  • Automate VPN setup with a CLI.
  • Written in Python/Qt5 for Linux, macOS, Windows.

For more information, including setup and configuration instructions, bug reports, and feature requests, head over to GitHub.
